About Vegan Recipes

Hello, Vegan Recipes is pleased to welcome you.

For this project, I wanted to create a cooking site with MEAN technology (MongoDB, ExpressJS, AngularJS and Node.js.) and Ionic for the mobile version. I took as the basis the site Recipesoriginales.fr.

I brought some novelties like locating a restaurant's vegan or a store's vegan. A page for all the publication on instagram are visible. As a user (if you log in) you will be able to publish your recipes and note those of the other members.

I hope you enjoy this site. I wish you a good journey ;-)
